Chapter Five

The Discovery

Research that matters

Conference Paper ICISD26-408

Fake Job Posting and Online Recruitment Scam Detection Utilizing Machine Learning and Advanced Natural Language Processing Techniques

International Conference on Intelligent System And Digital Transformation (ICISD'26)

SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Vadapalani Campus, Chennai · April 6-7, 2026

Abstract

This research proposes a hybrid neuro-symbolic fraud detection system that combines DistilBERT contextual embeddings, Isolation Forest anomaly detection, and a stacking ensemble of supervised classifiers to identify fraudulent job postings with high precision.

The study addresses the critical challenge of class imbalance (95:5 ratio) in the EMSCAD dataset. The system achieves 99% accuracy and effectively detects zero-day scams through unsupervised anomaly detection.
